Description of Paris:

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France. It has been one of the world's major centers of finance, diplomacy, commerce, culture, fashion, and gastronomy. For its leading role in the arts and sciences, as well as its early and extensive system of street lighting, in the 19th century, it became known as the City of Light.

Why I Like Paris?

I like Paris as it has many stunning sights that showcase impressive architecture and design, such as The Eiffel Tower and The Louvre Museum. Besides, Paris is also famous for its amazing French food and the tourists can enjoy eating in the traditional café.

Top 5 things to do in Paris:

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower for iconic views.
  • Explore the Louvre Museum for art treasures.
  • Stroll along the Seine River for romantic ambiance.
  • Experience the charm of Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur.
  • Indulge in French cuisine at local cafes and bistros.
